Arrival & Parking

The easiest way to get here is by car, as public transport in the area is limited. There is plenty of parking space on site. If you travel by train: there is a direct train to Orvieto from both Rome and Florence. From Orvieto it is about a 20-minute drive to our house. Please organise your journey yourself. We are happy to help you find a lift with other guests. In the questionnaire, please indicate whether you are coming by car or need support.
